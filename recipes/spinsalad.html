Berry Spinach Salad

This is an easy and versatile salad that is sure to impress the guests!

Ingredients

  • baby spinach
  • fresh blueberries
  • fresh strawberries
  • walnuts (or pecans)
  • dried cranberries
  • goat cheese (or feta cheese if you prefer something milder)
  • avacodo
  • red onion, julienned
  • raspberry vinaigrette
  • cracked black pepper

Instruction

  1. It's a salad! Mix it up as you see fit!
  2. Skip the cheese for a vegan-friendly recipe
  3. You can swap the vigairette for olive oil and honey

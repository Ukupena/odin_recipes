No-Bake Cookies

An easy, classic, go-to dessert for those that are into chocolatey, peanutbuttery, and tasty morsels!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 ts vanilla extract
  • 3 cups quick-cooking oats

Instructions

  1. Line 2 large baking sheets with parchment paper and set aside
  2. Combine butter, sugar, milk, and unsweetened cocoa in large saucepan. Heat over medium heat, stirring often until butter is melted and everything is combined. Bring mixture to a boil for 1 minute, stirring occasionally.
  3. Remove from heat and stir in peanut butter and vanilla extract.
  4. Stir in oats and mix until everything is well combined
  5. Drop large spoonfuls of mixture (or use a cooke scoop) onto prepared baking sheets
  6. Cool in refrigerator for about 20 minutes (or don't) and serve!

Goulash

A classic family favorite, this quick and easy to make recipe will have everyone coming back for more. A tasty blend of beef, egg noodles, and a savory tomato sauce is sure to leave everyone satisfied.

Ingredients

  • 1 (12oz) package of egg noddles
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, choipped
  • 3 cloves (or 1Tb) of minced garlic
  • 1 can of petite, diced tomatos
  • 2 (12oz) can of tomato sauce
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 Tb italian seasoning
  • salt & pepper to taste
  1. Preaheat oven to 350 degrees
  2. Cook egg noodles 2-3 minutes less than indicated on package instructions
  3. Heat large, oven-safe skillet to medium high heat and cook your ground beef and chopped onions together
  4. Drain fat from ground beef
  5. Add garlic to beef and onions and saute until fragrant (1-2 min)
  6. Add seasonings, tomato sauce, and diced potatoes
  7. Stir in cheddar cheese
  8. Place in oven for 15 minutes, or until cheese startes to bubble
